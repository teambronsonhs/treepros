🌿 When to Prune Trees in Essex

A seasonal guide for healthy trees — by Tree Pros, local arborists.

📅 Pruning Calendar for Essex & Suffolk

Jan-Mar
Oak, Ash, Maple (dormant season)
Apr-Jun
Cherry, Birch, Walnut (avoid oak)
Jul-Sep
Light pruning only — growing season
Oct-Dec
Conifers, evergreens, deadwood removal

🌳 Specific Tree Pruning Times

  • Oak trees: December - February (avoid Oak Wilt season)
  • Apple & Pear: November - March (winter dormancy)
  • Cherry & Plum: July - August (to avoid silver leaf disease)
  • Conifers (Leylandii): April - September (active growth)
  • Birch & Walnut: Late summer (avoid sap bleeding)
  • Dead or diseased branches: Any time of year
⚠️ Important: Some trees in Conservation Areas or with Tree Preservation Orders (TPO) require council permission before pruning. We handle all TPO applications for you.

✂️ Signs Your Tree Needs Pruning

  • Dead, broken, or hanging branches
  • Branches touching power lines or roof
  • Dense canopy blocking light to garden
  • Diseased or insect-infested areas
  • Crossing or rubbing branches
